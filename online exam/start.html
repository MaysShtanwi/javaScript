Front End Exam

The exam comprises three sections worth a total of 30 points:

Section 1 – HTML Multiple Choice (100 points)
Section 2 – CSS Multiple Choice (100 points)
Section 3 – JavaScript Multiple Choice (100 points)

The Multiple Choice section consists of a series of questions worth 10 points each. Three choices are presented with one of them being the correct answer. Each section assesses your knowledge of basic to advanced Front End concepts.
