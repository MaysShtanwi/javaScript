Front End Exam

Assess your Front End aptitude

The exam comprises two sections worth a total of 100 points: Section 1 – Multiple Choice (40 points) Section 2 – Written Answer (60 points)

The Multiple Choice section consists of a series of questions worth 1 point each. Five choices (A to E) are presented with one of them being the correct answer. This section assesses your knowledge of basic to advanced JavaScript concepts.
